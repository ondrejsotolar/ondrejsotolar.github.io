Hello, I am Ondřej Sotolář...

Ph.D. candidate AI researcher based in Brno, Czech Republic, EU.

I'm pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science. My research area is Natural Language Processing (NLP) with focus on: tool-assisted Large Language Models, detection of psychological phenomena in dialogue, and personal data anonymization.

In parallel, I work as a researcher in the Interdisciplinary Research Team on Internet and Society, where I conduct research and design the software for conducting experiments. To collect data for the latest research project, I developed an Android app, Java backend services, Web applications, a desktop application, and I manage PostgreSQL databases.

I am married and have two children, a 3yo and a toddler. We live in Zastávka near the city of Brno (Czech Republic, EU), where I work.
